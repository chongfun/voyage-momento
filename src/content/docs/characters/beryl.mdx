## Trinkets

:::note
Any trinket with an [Instant] ability is useful to allow Beryl to cast [BOOOOOM!] after moving. These trinkets are suggested because they do not require a nearby target, but you can use other trinkets such as [Flying Blade Armguard] if you'll have a valid target (e.g. a barrel near your desired destination).
