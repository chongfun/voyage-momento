## Tarot Whispers

<HorizontalCard>
    <Image src={tarot_devil} alt="Temptation of The Devil" class="object-contain" />
    <div class="flex flex-col justify-between p-4 leading-normal">
        ##### Temptation of The Devil

        For each <span class="text-orange-500">1</span> character defeated in battle, gains <span class="text-orange-500">1</span> stack of [<span class="text-blue-500">Devil</span>]. The effect lasts until the end of the battle and cannot be dispelled or immunized. [<span class="text-blue-500">Devil</span>] is a buff that increases ATK and DEF by <span class="text-orange-500">3%</span>, up to <span class="text-orange-500">30%</span>.

        Exclusive effect: Gains <span class="text-orange-500">2</span> stacks of [<span class="text-blue-500">Devil</span>] instead of <span class="text-orange-500">1</span>.
    </div>
</HorizontalCard>

        HP is increased by <span class="text-green-500">10%</span>. When initiating an active attack, if the character's max HP is greater than the target's, DMG dealt is increased by <span class="text-orange-500">10%</span>.

        Exclusive effect: HP and DMG dealt is increased by an additional <span class="text-green-500">10%</span>.

        *Obtained from the Tower of Adversity or Unnamed Legendary Tarot Whisper boxes.*
