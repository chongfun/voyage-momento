## Skills

:::note
At RK7 [Flagellant] enables shield spam, but [Invulnerable] doesn't cost NRG and is often used in [Weaponry Trial 2](/guides/weaponry_trial_2/). If you choose [Invulnerable] over [Flagellant], then choose [Defense (Ranged Attack)] over [Endurance Activation] at RK5 because the damage reduction buffs do not stack.
:::
