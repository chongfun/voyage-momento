## Gear Exchange Shop

### Radiant Crystal Nucleus

Radiant Crystal Nucleus is obtained from the breakdown of Legendary gear. You also get 500 
Radiant Crystal Nucleus from level 5 of the free track on the Cornucopia battle pass. It should be used to purchase Random Legendary Trinkets. Players who have unlocked legendary weapons from the Weaponry Trials (1 guaranteed per day from Stage 11) should be able to break down enough weapons to buy the two random trinkets every month.

Here's my personal ranking of the least useful weapons.

<Steps>
1. Beheading Machete (common drop)
2. Doomsday Scythe (common drop)
3. Denial Hammer (common drop)
4. Skeleton Staff (common drop)
5. Meteor Line (common drop)
6. Pendant Halberd (uncommon drop)
7. Hollow Axe (uncommon drop)
8. Guardian's Will (uncommon drop)
</Steps>

:::caution
Legendary weapons do not drop at the same rate; the following are rare weapons that you may want to keep even if you don't use them.

* Feast Axe
* Tuning Hammer
* Star-Raid Crossbow
* Longbow of Martyrs
* Skeleton Spear
* Nameless Staff
* Ever-Burning Blade
* Senator Swift Sword
* Focus Staff
* Resentment Broom
:::

:::note
Legendary trinkets are very scarce, so newer players should consider breaking down any unused legendary weapons if it'll get you enough Radiant Crystal Nucleus to buy a legendary trinket before the inventory refreshes at the end of the month; you'll eventually get more of the weapons.
:::

### Radiant Crystal Dust

Radiant Crystal Dust is obtained from the breakdown of Epic gear or lower.

:::note
If you're a lower-level player (pre-55), you're likely constrained by Radiant Powder and should buy as much as you need to get your core team's gear to level 60. However, Elysium Coins are a bottleneck for endgame engravings and talents, so buy the monthly 20000 Elysium Coins if you don't need more Radiant Powder.
:::

## Reputation Exchange Shop

### Prestige Crown

This currency comes from events such as the Elysium Brawl. We don't get enough to buy out the available items, so I'd only buy the unfarmable Random Legendary Tarot Whisper (Series II).

<Steps>
1. Random Legendary Tarot Whisper (Series II)
</Steps>

### Proof of Reputation

You get this currency from friends (1000/day) and guilds (9000/week if your guild hits max weekly activity) as well as some events. I've had enough currency to buy the following every inventory refresh.

<Steps>
1. Bond Charm
2. Radiant Powder (only if you still need it; endgame accounts probably don't)
3. Legendary Radiant Ore
4. Unnamed Legendary Engraving Chest
5. Unnamed Epic Engraving Chest
6. Elysium Coins
</Steps>

## Memory Crystal Exchange Shop

This currency comes from summoning characters that have been ascended to 5 stars. I've gotten ~14000 memory crystals at ~400 summons; this will increase the more you summon since you'll be more likely summon copies of fully-ascended characters. I predict that it'll take around a year to save enough memory crystals to buy the Legendary Character Selection (70000 memory crystals) as a F2P player. I personally have been buying the monthly discounted 5 Secret Fate (5000 memory crystals) instead of waiting that long to select a launch character.

## Insignia Exchange Shop

You get this currency from the Tower of Conquest weekly. If you've unlocked the max rewards (cleared floor 10), then you'll get 1300 insignia for clearing 9-5 every week. I prioritize buying the following items.

<Steps>
1. Random Legendary Trinket
2. Random Legendary Gear
3. Random Legendary Weapon
4. Radiant Powder (only if you still need it; endgame accounts probably don't)
5. Legendary Radiant Ore
6. Unnamed Stars
7. Unnamed Legendary Engraving Chest
8. Unnamed Epic Engraving Chest
9.  Elysium Coins
10. Tarot Essence
11. Star Particles
</Steps>

## Memorial Exchange Shop

If you're a newer player, you should prioritize furniture until you reach the max bond bonus (1000 coziness). You do not need the place the furniture to get the coziness.
