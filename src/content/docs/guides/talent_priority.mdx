<Steps>

1. Tactics
   * Destroyer: Ragnarok, Maneuver Command
   * Seeker: March Command
   * Breaker: Summon Explosive Barrel
   * Watcher: Urgent Order, Flag of Convallaria, War Horn
   * Defender: Pursuit Order, Invincible Hero
2. Initial TP & TP Recovery
3. P.ATK & M.ATK
4. Enhancement of All Allies (unlocked at Platinum)
   * Destroyer: M.ATK
   * Seeker: P.ATK
   * Breaker: HP
   * Watcher: M.DEF
   * Defender: P.DEF
5. Role-specific buffs

</Steps>

:::note
You should spread out your star materials to progress each path to unlock talent nodes, but you can focus on the paths that you use the most after you max the tactic and TP nodes at the Gold tier of each path.
:::
