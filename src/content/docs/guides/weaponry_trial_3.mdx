Unlike WT1 and WT2, this boss does not punish melee or ranged damage. However, you will want to bring physical DPS (and magical DPS in Stage 11) to break the boss's shield.

Boss pattern:

<Steps>

1. Attacks with [Ashes to Ashes] and summons a turret
2. Attacks with [Omen of Doom] targeting the character with the highest M.DEF 
3. Generates a physical shield (alternates between magical & physical in Stage 11) with [Misfortune Cohesion] and summons turret(s)
4. Attacks with [Misfortune Cohesion] AoE (enhanced damage if the shield is intact) and summons turret(s)
5. Attacks with [Apocalypse] AoE
6. Repeat from ②

</Steps>

:::tip
This boss gets a 4% damage buff stack for each turret killed. Ensure that you dispel these stacks regularly. The [Redemption of The Judgment] tarot whisper or the [Tuning Hammer] axe are good options.
:::

:::tip
The first [Apocalypse] AoE from the boss before you have many [<span class="text-blue-500">Devil</span>] stacks is the most dangerous. Underlevelled strategies rely on stacking debuffs (e.g. Outlaw Guard's [Intimidate] debuff, Gloria's [Solemnity] aura) and buffs (e.g. Gloria's [Longinus - Brilliance] buff, Angel's [M.DEF Command] aura) to survive.
:::

:::note
The character with the highest M.DEF may not always be the same throughout the fight due to [<span class="text-blue-500">Devil</span>] tarot whisper stacks, Angel's MDEF aura, Angel's random MDEF buff from healing, Gloria's flag range, etc. 
:::

:::caution
Like the other weaponry trial bosses, this boss gets an extra turn when it kills a unit. Fragile summons such as Inanna's Guard are a liability.
:::

## Stage 11 - Level 70

:::tip
In Stage 11, the boss is immune to debuffs while it has a shield. Use your [Disaster Bottle] or [War Horn] when it doesn't have a shield.
:::
