## Memory Retrieval

You can farm up to three memory shards per character in Crossing World's Memory Retrieval stages (at the last stage, you get 9 shards a day).

| Stars | Shards | Days | Cumulative |
| ----- | ------ | ---- | ---------- |
| 2 | 20 | 6 ⅔ | 6 ⅔ |
| 3 | 60 | 20 | 26 ⅔ |
| 4 | 100 | 33 ⅓ | 60 |
| 5 | 120 | 40 | 100 |

## PVE Priority

Some characters have star breakpoints that significantly improve their kit. Your farming slots will vary based on your roster (e.g. I wouldn't farm Garcia if I had Col). Unless you pull for dupes, you'll likely never have free slots to push characters past the recommended breakpoints.

:::note
I've only ordered the characters released up to Caris, but some upcoming characters will have higher priority so you'll likely have to swap out in-progress characters if you summon them.
:::
