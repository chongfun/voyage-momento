This trial punishes melee damage. Ranged attacks from melee dps are fine (e.g. [Gloria](/characters/gloria)'s [Lance of Longinus] or [Sword - Full Moon]). Bring AOE heals and debuff cleansing for stigmata stacks.

The boss has a three turn cycle:

<Steps>

1. Attacks the closest unit with a single-target [Dust to Dust] attack
2. Charges a frontal AOE
3. After the frontal AOE lands, charges a 3x3 AOE targeting the lowest HP% unit.

</Steps>

:::tip
The damage from the 3x3 AOE can be split between up to 3 units. Magic tanks such as [Suppression](/characters/suppression) can tank it alone, or you can intentionally lower the HP of sacrificial units (e.g. Guarding Dummy Deployment, [Inanna](/characters/inanna)'s guard) in stigmata tiles so that they're targeted for the 3x3 AOE.
:::

:::caution
If the boss kills a unit, it gets an extra turn.
:::

## Stage 11 - Level 70

:::tip
In Stage 11, [Dust to Dust] will now mark the target with an undispellable debuff that will trigger an AOE if the target is hit again.  Rotate who takes the hit or use a sacrifice such as Guarding Dummy Deployment or Acambe's summon.
:::
