This trial punishes ranged damage, but AOE attacks are fine.

The boss begins the battle by debuffing the two farthest targets with no healing. It then repeats the following pattern:

<Steps>

1. Attacks and knockbacks the target farthest from it
2. Attacks the two closest targets (inflicting a thunder debuff that will do piercing damage in a cross shape on the subsequent turn)
3. Attacks with frontal knockback AOE and spawns rocks that will explode if they're hit with piercing damage

</Steps>

:::tip
Papal Guard is amazing for tanking the nuke in (1). He can sustain himself without support from your healer when equipped with the Cooling Powder trinket and the Gibbous Moon tarot whisper.
:::

:::caution
If the boss kills a unit, it gets an extra turn.
:::

## Stage 11 - Level 70

:::tip
In Stage 11, the rocks no longer explode from merely being hit with piercing damage. You'll need to balance destroying the rocks and attacking the boss to avoid being wiped by the boss exploding the rocks. You can let the cross shape attack hit the rocks to do some damage before you finish them off.
:::
