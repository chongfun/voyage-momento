:::note
Tarot whispers with a legendary 4th row have a chance of rolling an exclusive effect. These will be the best endgame pieces, so I wouldn't bother leveling any tarots that don't have a legendary 4th row (apart from the initial tarots you level for your team in the early game).
:::
